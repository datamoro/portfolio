Enterprise BI Transformation

19% Volume Growth

The Challenge

The operational team relied on disjointed Excel reports, leading to data silos and reactive decision-making rather than proactive strategy. There was no single source of truth for logistics KPIs.

The Solution

Built the department's first BI ecosystem from scratch with 30+ automated dashboards. Conducted deep-dive logistics zoning studies for key accounts like Electrolux, optimizing delivery routes and costs.

Key Achievements

  • Designed and deployed 30+ Power BI reports, becoming the standard for 50+ daily users.
  • Enabled a 19% growth in volume for a key logistics account through data-driven route optimization.
  • Automated data ingestion using Power Query and SQL, reducing manual reporting time by 90%.
